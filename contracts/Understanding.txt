# ChitX Blockchain Implementation — Simple Explanation

This document explains how our blockchain system works in plain and easy language.

## 1. What is the CTX Token? (ChitXToken.sol)
Think of the CTX Token as the "Digital Currency" created specifically for our platform.
- **Supply**: There are 1,000,000 CTX tokens to start with.
- **Treasury**: All these tokens are stored in a "Treasury" (the platform's main wallet).
- **Purpose**: We use these tokens to give rewards (Airdrops) to new users and to pay out winners from the pools.

## 2. What is the ChitPool? (ChitPool.sol)
The ChitPool is like a "Digital Locker" or "Safe" where everyone's money is kept during a chit fund cycle.
- **Escrow**: When you join a pool and pay your monthly fee, your CTX tokens go inside this safe.
- **Security**: No human can just take the money. The tokens are locked until the "AI Oracle" decides who should get them.
- **Payouts**: Once the AI calculates a winner (based on need and trust), it tells the Safe to automatically send the money to that winner's wallet.

## 3. How the Backend Connects (web3Service.js)
The backend acts as the bridge between our code and the Polygon Blockchain.
- **Automatic Airdrops**: When you verify your bank statement, the backend automatically sends you CTX tokens based on your Trust Score.
- **Verification**: It checks the blockchain to make sure you actually have enough tokens before you join a pool.
- **Gas Fees**: We use the Polygon Amoy Testnet because it is extremely fast and the transaction fees are almost zero.

## 4. Key Terms Simplified
- **Wallet Address**: Your unique digital ID on the blockchain (like a bank account number).
- **Smart Contract**: A piece of code that lives on the blockchain and handles money automatically without a middleman.
- **Wei**: The smallest unit of a token (like pennies to a dollar). We handle large numbers because the blockchain counts every tiny fraction of a token.

---
This system ensures that your money is safe, payouts are fair, and everything is tracked transparently on the blockchain.
